iOS is at the core of every iPhone and iPad. Advanced features enable iOS devices to be deployed seamlessly and securely in your business. Powerful apps give users the freedom to create, communicate, and collaborate from anywhere. And all with the ease of use that Apple is known for.

iOS is designed to deliver everything business users need to do their best work wherever they are. With a simple, intuitive interface, powerful built-in features, and rock-solid stability, iOS provides the foundation to drive productivity in game-changing ways.

You know how to use iOS devices from the first time you pick them up. That’s because the revolutionary Multi‑Touch interface in iOS was designed for your finger. The device gets out of the way to let you immerse yourself in the task at hand. From built-in apps to thousands of apps ready for work in the App Store. From asking Siri to schedule a meeting to video conferencing with FaceTime. Everything is easy, intuitive, and familiar.

A completely integrated approach to product design brings unique features to life. Hardware and software made for each other create a deeply engaging experience that lets you feel like you’re interacting with the data, not a piece of technology. You can share a contact with a nearby colleague in a few taps using AirDrop. Present ideas to a group using AirPlay Mirroring and AppleTV. Let remote colleagues see what you see using FaceTime. Only on iOS.

An integrated approach enables iOS to have excellent support for software updates. This means that as software evolves and new features are added, updates are immediately available to all devices, bringing new functionality for users and IT departments. All iOS software updates are made available for free, on the same day across a wide variety of supported devices and models.

iOS provides IT with powerful, yet simple ways to deploy and manage iOS devices, secure corporate data, and enable a great user experience.

iOS is designed with advanced security technologies that provide end-to-end control of devices, data, and apps. Many security features are enabled by default and require no configuration. Others are managed easily to fit seamlessly into your organization without added complexity for users. iOS delivers a secure architecture, provides enhanced data protection, and offers seamless access to corporate information networks.

From ten devices to ten thousand, corporate-owned or BYOD, deploying iPhone and iPad devices in your organization can be a zero-touch effort for IT. iOS makes it simple for employees to set up their devices, right out of the box. They can activate them, configure basic settings, enroll in corporate services, and start working right away — all without requiring help from IT.

iOS provides all the tools you need to set up, deploy, and manage iOS devices without compromising the user experience. Thanks to iOS and built-in frameworks for third-party mobile device management (MDM) solutions, you can manage devices over the air and configure IT services like corporate email, Wi-Fi, VPN, and certificates.

Learn how iOS provides IT stakeholders with powerful technologies to secure, deploy, and manage iPhone and iPad devices with end-to-end control while enhancing the user experience.

iOS has a complete ecosystem for apps. From off-the-shelf apps in the App Store to custom apps created in-house or by a third-party developer, you have infinite ways to transform your business.

iOS and an ever-expanding collection of features and built-in apps make iPhone and iPad even more powerful, innovative, and enjoyable to use for work. Apps like Mail, Contacts, Calendar, Reminders, and Notes offer the business professional everything they need to work on the go and never miss a business opportunity.

The App Store is the place to find off-the-shelf apps for almost any business task. Purchase apps in volume and easily distribute apps throughout your entire organization. Users get a great experience and IT can trust that all apps are reviewed by Apple to ensure they operate as described. Simply put, they just work.

iOS is the perfect platform for in-house app development. Take advantage of world-class developer resources provided by Apple to help you create apps that differentiate your business. With custom apps you can automate workflows, securely access corporate content, build compelling customer solutions, and more.

There’s an app to make just about everything you do even better. Off-the-shelf App Store apps and custom-built apps offer endless opportunities to get a fresh, new start to work.
